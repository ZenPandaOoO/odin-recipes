Curry

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 jar of your favorite curry sauce
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Directions

  1. Brown the Chicken: In a large skillet or Dutch Oven over medium heat, brown the chicken pieces on all sides. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  2. Sate the aromatics: Add the onion and garlic to the skillet and saute until softened and fragrant, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add the curry sauce in the skillet and stir to coat the chicken and vegetables
  4. Simmer: Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce heat to low and simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through,
  5. Add Vegetable Broth and Coconut Milk: If desired, add vegetable broth and cocount milk for a creamier and more flavorful curry.
  6. Season to Taste: Taste the curry and adjust the seasonings as needed. Add more salt, pepper, or additional curry sauce to suit your preferences.
  7. Garnish and Serve: Once the curry is ready, rgarnish with fresh cilantro and serve over rice or naan bread.
